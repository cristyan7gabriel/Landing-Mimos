# **App Name**: Mimos Landing

## Core Features:

- Product Showcase: Interactive carousel to display handmade products with high-quality images and brief descriptions.
- Interactive Banner: Animated banner with seasonal promotions and special offers.
- Contact Form: Simple form for users to send inquiries.
- About Section: Information about 'Mimos Doces e Presentes' and the story behind the handmade products.
- AI Product Suggestion Tool: Integrate AI to provide gift recommendations based on user-provided preferences. The AI tool will present relevant products available for purchase.
- Instagram Feed: Display the company's instagram feed.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft purple (#A080DD) for a gentle and inviting feel.
- Background color: Light off-white (#F5F3F7) to maintain a clean and simple design.
- Accent color: Pale gold (#D4AC0D) to highlight important sections and create a touch of luxury.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif to convey warmth; Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern serif with a high-end feel.
- Use elegant, handcrafted-style icons related to gifts and sweets.
- Maintain a clean and spacious layout to allow content to breathe and to ensure easy navigation.
- Use subtle animations on hover effects and carousel transitions to add a touch of interactivity.
